Training: Unlock

Make Your Web App Talk

Source code: https://github.com/thirstyhead/unlock

In this workshop, we'll explore the built-in speech capabilities of the modern browser. We'll discuss a11y (Accessibility), Speech Synthesis (TTS, or Text-to-Speech), and Speech Recognition.

  • HTML
  • A11y (Accessibility)
  • Speech Synthesis
  • SSML (Simple Speech Markup Language)
  • Speech Recognition
